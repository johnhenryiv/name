Quick Reference: Before You Meet
  • ✅
    Have the talk first. Discuss expectations, boundaries, and what you are and aren't comfortable with before any in-person meetup.
  • 🏥
    Get tested regularly. STI testing every 3-6 months (or before new partners) is a responsible practice. Many clinics offer free or low-cost confidential testing.
  • 🛡️
    Use protection. Condoms and other barrier methods significantly reduce STI transmission and unintended pregnancy risk.
  • 🤝
    Consent is ongoing. Consent can be withdrawn at any time. Check in with your partner and respect their decisions without question.
  • 💬
    Communicate openly. If feelings change or the arrangement no longer works, have an honest conversation. Honesty prevents hurt feelings down the road.
  • 🧘
    Check in with yourself. FWB arrangements can be fulfilling when boundaries are clear. If you start feeling confused or hurt, it's okay to reassess.
